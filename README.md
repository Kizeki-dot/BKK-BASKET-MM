 # 🛒 BKK Basket MM

A modern landing page for **BKK Basket MM**, a Thailand-to-Myanmar shopping and cargo service.

🌐 Live Website: https://bkk-basket-mm.netlify.app

---

## 📖 About

BKK Basket MM helps customers in Myanmar purchase products from Thailand and receive them through reliable cargo and shopping support services.

The website is designed to provide:

- Thailand product purchasing assistance
- Bangkok address service
- Air cargo shipping support
- Easy order submission
- Facebook contact integration
- Responsive experience across devices

---
